        Welcome to Lift Tracker

        Set up your training

        A training split is the way exercises are divided across workout days. For example, Push/Pull/Legs groups pushing muscles, pulling muscles and lower-body exercises on separate days.

        Push/Pull/Legs (PPL) is widely used because it is easy to understand, covers the whole body and is a good starting point for beginners. You can edit every day and exercise later.

        How progress estimates work

        Estimated 1RM predicts a possible one-repetition maximum from a lighter set. Treat it as a progress estimate—not a requirement to attempt a heavy lift. Training volume is weight × repetitions and is most useful when comparing similar exercises.
